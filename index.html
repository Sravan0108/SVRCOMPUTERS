About Us

Welcome to SVR Computer Education, your trusted partner in mastering the world of technology. Founded with the mission to provide high-quality computer education, we have been empowering students, professionals, and enthusiasts with the skills they need to thrive in the digital age.


Our Mission

At SVR Computer Education, our mission is to deliver comprehensive and accessible computer education that prepares our students for the demands of the IT industry. We aim to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application, ensuring that our learners are industry-ready.


Our Vision

Our vision is to become a leading institution in computer education, recognized for our commitment to excellence and innovation. We strive to foster a community of lifelong learners who are equipped to adapt to the ever-evolving technological landscape.


What We Offer

We offer a wide range of courses designed to meet the diverse needs of our students. Whether you're a beginner looking to learn the basics or a professional seeking to enhance your skills, we have a course for you. Our offerings include:

  • Web Development: Learn to build modern, responsive websites using the latest technologies.
  • Graphic Design: Master the art of visual communication with our comprehensive design courses.
  • Programming Languages: Get proficient in popular programming languages such as Python, Java, C++, and more.
  • Data Science: Dive into data analysis, machine learning, and big data technologies.
  • Networking: Understand the fundamentals of networking and network security.

Office Automation Skills

DCA/PGDCA

Programming Courses

Learn programming with our expert-led courses! From Python to JavaScript, gain practical skills and build real-world projects. Start coding today and open doors to exciting tech careers!.

Data Base

Master Database Management with our course! Learn SQL, database design, data manipulation, and advanced querying techniques. Build a strong foundation for managing and analyzing data, perfect for aspiring data analysts and IT professionals!

Tally+GST

Learn Tally with our expert-led course! Gain hands-on experience in accounting, GST, inventory management, and financial reporting. Perfect for aspiring accountants and business professionals!.

Full Stack Web Development

Become a Full Stack Developer with our comprehensive course! Learn front-end technologies like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and back-end tools such as Node.js, databases, and more. Build dynamic, full-fledged web applications and kickstart your career in tech!.

Learn Software Testing with our hands-on course! Understand key concepts like manual testing, automation, bug tracking, and quality assurance. Equip yourself with the skills to ensure software reliability and performance, ideal for aspiring testers and QA professionals!.

Adobe Photoshop

AI & Data Science
